gliderpro 2008-06-01 10:18 #1 Rev.: 590

Added a new mode to the freighter bot: the scavenger.
The scavenger will warp to a belt bookmark, warp to a wreck, loot the wreck, and move on.
It does not look for other player or NPC ships.
NPC ships are usually not a problem because it will get in and out before they do any serious damage.
Use a fast ship with a decent cargohold. For example, a Prober with a cargo extender, micro aux. power control, small shield extender, and medium shield extender seems to work well.
